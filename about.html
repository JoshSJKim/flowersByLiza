She is deeply inspired by nature, captivated by interesting textures, and motivated by vibrant colors. Although she hasn't received any formal training in floral arrangements, her background in theater production gives her an extraordinary ability to envision the clients' wedding, and bring to life the most memorable "stage" for the bride and groom (as well as the guests).

Services Offered

Liza will get to know each soon-to-be bride and groom on a personal level in order to develop an understanding of their preferred style and interests.

General services include:

  • Initial consultation
  • Event/venue design
  • Delivery and setup
  • On-site cleanup and pickup

Cherry Blossom is capable of curating a wide range of arrangement styles, including:

  • Glamorous
  • Romantic
  • Rustic/Vintage
  • Classic (French/English)
  • Modern
  • Country

Arrangements are not limited to just bouquets and centerpieces. Cherry Blossom also provides aisle decor, chuppah decor, table arrangements, arches, boutonnieres, corsages, etc.
Clients are encouraged to be specific and descriptive during consultation.
