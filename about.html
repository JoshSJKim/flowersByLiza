Cherry Blossom Floral Studio is a professional wedding floral design service based in Mississauga, Ontario. Liza Heejeong Chun, the owner of the studio, discovered her passion for wedding floral arrangements after designing beautiful pieces for her sister’s wedding. She is deeply inspired by nature and captivated by interesting textures and vibrant colors. Cherry Blossom Floral Studio will focus on the finer details, designing pieces that represent the essence of each couple’s love story.

Services Offered

Liza will get to know each soon-to-be-newlywed on a personal level, developing an understanding of their preferred style and tastes. Clients have the opportunity to avail of initial consultations, delivery, setup, and cleanup services at their desired venue. Cherry Blossom Floral Studio has the ability to curate a wide spectrum of floral styles such as glamorous, romantic, and rustic pieces. There is a variety of arrangements on offer including aisle decor, boutonnieres, corsages, chuppah decor, and table arrangements.
